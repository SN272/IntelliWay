🧭 About IntelliWay
Smart. Sustainable. Inclusive. Indian.

🌟 What Is IntelliWay?

An AI-powered eco-mobility platform for real-time, multimodal, and inclusive route planning Designed for all citizens — including semi-urban users, low-literate individuals, and differently-abled commuters Seamlessly integrates metro, e-bikes, walking paths, and buses with live data from weather, traffic, and disaster APIs Built for Bharat — available in local languages, usable offline, and accessible across device types

🚀 Why We Built IntelliWay (The Opportunity)

Mobility is evolving, but gaps still exist in inclusive design, eco-conscious routing, and disaster resilience.
India needs a mobility assistant that works beyond Tier-1 cities and adapts to real-world constraints. IntelliWay addresses this by using AI, ML, and voice-first UI to create a truly inclusive and scalable solution. Aligns with UN Sustainable Development Goals:
1.SDG 9 (Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure)
2.SDG 11 (Sustainable Cities & Communities)
3.SDG 13 (Climate Action)

🎯 Our Vision

To democratize intelligent travel by making green, adaptive, and inclusive mobility accessible to all.
To empower every commuter to move safely, sustainably, and confidently — regardless of literacy, ability, or geography.
To contribute meaningfully to India’s Smart City Mission and global climate action goals

✨ Key Features

  • 🌱 Eco Routing – Suggests routes with the lowest environmental impact using public transport, walking, and e-bikes
  • 📡 Live Data Integration – Real-time weather, traffic, and disaster-aware routing
  • 🧠 ML-Personalization – Learns user patterns to recommend smarter, faster, context-aware travel options
  • 🎙️ Voice + Icon Interface – Designed for non-literate and multilingual users with intuitive voice and visual controls
  • ♿ Inclusive Accessibility Wheelchair-friendly navigation and simplified UI for users of all abilities
  • 📶 Offline Support – Saved routes and map previews available without internet access
